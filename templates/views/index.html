About us

Covid -19 in 2019 killed millions of people worldwide, shocking and igniting the entire globe. All social interactions and daily activities were halted during the lockdown, leaving everyone helpless, disabled, and anxious about what might occur. The question of what can be done with this waste to limit landfills, protect our mother earth from serious environmental problems, and find value in it arose as a result of the rise in waste production in our nation. The inspiration for Trash to Cash came at that time. As people were already anxious due to the pandemic, efficient garbage collection and segregation was urgently needed.

What is Our Role?

Best Trash Managers

We are a social enterprise that aims to assist people in their busy lives by providing convenient and free pickup service by just scheduling us through our smartphone. As a recycling waste collector, we provide doorstep collection services for private residences, societies, businesses, and other entities. Customers' recyclable products are gathered by us, recycled into raw materials, and sold to various businesses for use in the production of new goods.
